TIPS

Eat 5-6 times a day

You should every 2 or 3 hours. consume 200-1200 calories in every meal, depending on your goal.

Proteins

One gram of protein contains 4 calries. physically active people should eat at least 2 grams of protein per kg of their body weight. that will ensure their muscle maintenance.

Carbohydrates

One gram of cabs has 4 calories. Because of your energy needs you should eat a good number of them. consume them from quality sources (oats,rice,potato) Experience with cabs: if you are naturally skinny and you have a tugh time gaining weight eat them in big quantities.

Fats

One gram of fat contains 9 calories. Don't skip them since our organism can't function properly if we don't eat them, but their consumption shouln't exceed 30% of your daily intake

Water

Because of that higher protein intake you should drink a lot of water so you don't get kidney problems. For every 100gms of protein drink at least 2| of water.

Supplements

You can read more on this topic in our application. we explained how every supplement should be used and if it works.

Alcohol

One gram of alcohol contains 7 calories! calories from alcohol don't contain any nutritive value so we call them empty calories. it also slows down your metabolism so with its consumation you will spend less calories.

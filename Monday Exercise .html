1- Bench Press

Reps : 10-12-12-10

Load : 70%

Speed : 80%

Rest : 90sec

Body Part : Chest

Description

Lay on a flate bench. Hold your glutes on the bench,and your feet on the floor. Arch your to activate lower chest more and that way lift a heavy weight.

  1. Take the barbell with your arms extended more than shoulder width.
  2. Take a deep breadth and slowly down the barbell on your chest.
  3. Return the barbell on the orignal positon and take breadth out.
